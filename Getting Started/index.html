Dogs have four legs, usually. They have an acute sense of smell, and a seeming fondness for humans, although it is unclear if this affection is born out of a genuine emotional response, or based on human brainwashing.

Dogs are descended from wolves, which are vicious creatures that work in small familial groups to murder large game incuding: deer, bison, caribou, small children, and occultists. Dogs however are generally pretty chill, and can be easily plyed with peanut butter.
